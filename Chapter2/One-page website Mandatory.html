Narcos

Overview

Narcos is a crime drama television series created by Chris Brancato, Carlo Bernard, and Doug Miro. The show explores the rise of the Medellín Cartel, led by Pablo Escobar, and the efforts of the Drug Enforcement Administration (DEA) to bring him down.

The first two seasons focus on Escobar's operations and eventual downfall, while the third season shifts to the rise of the Cali Cartel.

Characters

The series maainly follows Pablo Escobar, the infamous drug lord of the Medellín Cartel, and the DEA agents Steve Murphy and Javier Peña tasked with bringing him down.

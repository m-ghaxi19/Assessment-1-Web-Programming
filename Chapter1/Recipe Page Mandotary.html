Delicious Alfredo Pasta

Indulge in the creamy, rich flavors of this homemade Alfredo pasta — the perfect comfort food!

What You'll Need

  • 12 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 cup chopped parsley (for that fresh, herby touch)
  • 1 cup cooked chicken or shrimp (optional, but oh-so-delicious!)

How to Make It

  1. Start by bringing a big pot of salted water to a boil. Once it’s bubbling away, toss in your fettuccine pasta and cook it just until it’s al dente — firm but not too chewy. Drain and set it aside.
  2. If you're adding chicken, heat up some olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Season a chicken breast with salt and pepper, then cook it for 5-7 minutes per side until it's golden brown and fully cooked through. Slice it thinly, and set it aside to rest.
  3. In the same pan, melt your butter on medium heat. Keep an eye on it so it doesn’t burn! We want it to melt smoothly.
  4. Now add your garlic and sauté it for about a minute until it’s fragrant — trust me, your kitchen will smell amazing!
  5. Turn down the heat to low, and slowly pour in the heavy cream, stirring as it comes to a simmer. Let it gently bubble away for about 2-3 minutes.
  6. Slowly add the Parmesan cheese, stirring until it melts into the sauce and creates a creamy, luscious texture. Taste it! Add a little salt and pepper if you like.
  7. Time to add the pasta! Toss it into the sauce, making sure each strand is coated with that creamy goodness.
  8. If you’ve got chicken or shrimp, throw it in now and stir it all together to mix the flavors.
  9. Let everything simmer together for another minute or two to bring out all the deliciousness.
  10. Garnish with a sprinkle of fresh parsley to add a little color and freshness to your plate.
  11. Serve it up, and enjoy the creamy, cheesy heaven that is your Alfredo pasta!
